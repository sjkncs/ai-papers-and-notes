# HALP: Detecting Hallucinations in Vision-Language Models without Generating a Single Token

> **arXiv:** [2603.05465](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05465) | **日期 / Date:** 2026-03-05 | **作者 / Authors:** Sai Akhil Kogilathota, Sripadha Vallabha E G, Luzhe Sun, Jiawei Zhou

---

## 关键摘录 / Key Excerpts

> 1. "Hallucinations remain a persistent challenge for vision-language models (VLMs), which often describe nonexistent objects or fabricate facts."
>    / "幻觉仍然是视觉语言模型（VLM）的持久挑战，这些模型经常描述不存在的对象或捏造事实。"

> 2. "We investigate whether hallucination risk can instead be predicted before any token is generated by probing a model's internal representations in a single forward pass."
>    / "我们研究是否可以通过在单次前向传播中探测模型的内部表示，在任何 token 生成之前预测幻觉风险。"

> 3. "Probes trained on these representations achieve strong hallucination-detection performance without decoding, reaching up to 0.93 AUROC on Gemma-3-12B, Phi-4-VL 5.6B, and Molmo 7B."
>    / "在这些表示上训练的探针无需解码即可实现强大的幻觉检测性能，在 Gemma-3-12B、Phi-4-VL 5.6B 和 Molmo 7B 上达到最高 0.93 AUROC。"

> 4. "Late query-token states are the most predictive for most models, while visual or mid-layer features dominate in a few architectures."
>    / "对于大多数模型，后期 query-token 状态最具预测性，而在少数架构中，视觉或中间层特征占主导。"

> 5. "These results demonstrate that hallucination risk is detectable pre-generation, the most informative layer and modality vary across architectures, and lightweight probes have the potential to enable early abstention, selective routing, and adaptive decoding."
>    / "这些结果证明幻觉风险在生成前可检测，最具信息量的层和模态因架构而异，轻量级探针有潜力实现早期放弃、选择性路由和自适应解码。"

## Q1: 核心问题 / Core Problem

**中文：**

VLM 幻觉问题（描述不存在的对象或捏造事实）严重影响可靠性和安全性。现有检测方法在文本生成之后运行，干预成本高且不及时。本文核心问题是：**能否在模型生成任何文本 token 之前，仅通过单次前向传播探测内部表示来预测幻觉风险？**

**English:**

VLM hallucination (describing nonexistent objects or fabricating facts) severely impacts reliability and safety. Existing detection methods operate after text generation, making intervention costly and untimely. The core question is: **Can hallucination risk be predicted before generating any text token, by probing internal representations in a single forward pass?**

---

## Q2: 核心贡献 / Claimed Contributions

**中文：**

1. **生成前幻觉检测**：首次系统验证在 VLM 生成任何 token 之前预测幻觉风险的可行性。
2. **三类内部表示分析**：系统评估 (i) 纯视觉特征、(ii) 文本解码器中的视觉 token 表示、(iii) 融合视觉与文本的 query-token 表示。
3. **跨 8 款现代 VLM 验证**：在 Llama-3.2-Vision、Gemma-3、Phi-4-VL、Qwen2.5-VL 等模型上验证，最高达 0.93 AUROC。
4. **架构差异性发现**：后期 query-token 状态对大多数模型最具预测性，但不同架构的最优层和模态不同。
5. **实用前景**：为早期放弃、选择性路由和自适应解码提供了轻量级解决方案。

**English:**

1. **Pre-generation hallucination detection**: First systematic verification of predicting hallucination risk before VLM generates any token.
2. **Three families of internal representations**: Systematically evaluates (i) visual-only features, (ii) vision-token representations in the text decoder, (iii) query-token representations integrating vision and text.
3. **Validation across 8 modern VLMs**: Validated on Llama-3.2-Vision, Gemma-3, Phi-4-VL, Qwen2.5-VL, achieving up to 0.93 AUROC.
4. **Architecture-dependent findings**: Late query-token states are most predictive for most models, but optimal layers and modalities vary across architectures.
5. **Practical implications**: Provides lightweight solutions for early abstention, selective routing, and adaptive decoding.

## Q3: 审稿攻击点 / Reviewer Attack Points

**中文：**

1. **探针的泛化性**：在 A 数据集上训练的探针能否泛化到 B 数据集？是否存在严重的域偏移问题？
2. **因果 vs 相关**：内部表示中的幻觉信号是因果的还是仅仅是统计相关？
3. **计算开销**：虽然声称"单次前向传播"，但探测所有层的表示是否实际可行？
4. **与后生成检测的对比**：相比 SelfCheckGPT、VLM 自我验证等方法，预生成检测的优势是否量化清晰？
5. **假阳性/假阴性分析**：在高 AUROC 下，误判的具体案例和模式是否被分析？

**English:**

1. **Probe generalization**: Can probes trained on dataset A generalize to dataset B? Are there domain shift issues?
2. **Causal vs correlational**: Are hallucination signals in internal representations causal or merely statistically correlated?
3. **Computational overhead**: Although claiming "single forward pass," is probing all layers practically feasible?
4. **Comparison with post-generation detection**: Compared to SelfCheckGPT and VLM self-verification, are advantages of pre-generation detection clearly quantified?
5. **False positive/negative analysis**: Under high AUROC, are specific failure cases and patterns analyzed?

## Q4: 博士生阅读指南 / PhD Reading Guide

**中文：**

**如果你做 VLM 安全 / 可信 AI / 模型可解释性：**
- **精读**：实验方法论（如何构建探针）、三类表示的对比、跨模型分析。
- **复现**：在 Qwen2.5-VL 或 LLaVA 上提取各层表示，训练线性探针，比较 AUROC。
- **延伸**：将探针集成到推理流程中，实现自适应解码（检测到幻觉风险时切换到保守解码策略）。

**English:**

**If you work on VLM safety, trustworthy AI, or model interpretability:**
- **Read carefully**: experimental methodology (probe construction), comparison of three representation families, cross-model analysis.
- **Reproduce**: extract layer-wise representations on Qwen2.5-VL or LLaVA, train linear probes, compare AUROC.
- **Extend**: integrate probes into the inference pipeline for adaptive decoding (switch to conservative decoding when hallucination risk is detected).
